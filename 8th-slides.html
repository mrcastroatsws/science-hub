MON | DEFINITIONS

The Trinity of Circuits

1. Source

Provides the energy push.
(e.g., Battery, Outlet)

2. Receiver

Uses the energy to do work.
(e.g., Bulb, Motor, Phone)

3. Path

The road for electrons.
(e.g., Copper Wire)

The Short Circuit

PATH WITH NO RECEIVER = FIRE

THU | SYMBOLS

Schematic Language

Engineers don't draw "art". We draw logic.

Battery Symbol: Long line (+) Short line (-)
Wire Symbol: Straight Line
Bulb Symbol: Circle with X or Loop
Switch Symbol: Gate (Open/Closed)
