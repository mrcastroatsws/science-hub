Empathize

"It's Not About You"

Before you build, you must understand the User.

Application: Who is affected by the problem? Is it farmers? Doctors? Students?

The Interview

You cannot guess what people need. You must ask.

"Tell me about the last time you experienced [Problem]..."

Define

"The Problem Statement"

Data without direction is useless. You must craft a Point of View (POV).

User + Need + Insight

Mad Libs Statement

"_________ (The User) needs a way to _________ (The Need) because _________ (Surprising Insight)."

Ideate

"Quantity over Quality"

Brainstorming phase. No idea is "bad" yet. We want wild solutions.

Crazy 8s

Fold a paper into 8 sections. Sketch 8 different solutions in 8 minutes.

STOP JUDGING YOURSELF.

Prototype

"Build to Think"

A prototype is a question made physical. Your first experiment is a prototype.

Cardboard & Tape

Do not buy expensive materials yet. Build the concept using "trash."

Does it fit in the hand? Does it make sense?

Test

"Feedback is a Gift"

Find where it breaks. Show, don't tell. Hand the user the prototype and say nothing.

Feedback Matrix

What worked?
What failed?
Questions?
New Ideas?
