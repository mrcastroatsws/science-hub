Autotrophs

Make their own food (Plants, Algae).

Heterotrophs

Consume others for food (Humans, Fungi).

FRI | OVERVIEW

Cellular Respiration

6O₂ + C₆H₁₂O₆ → 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + Energy

Oxygen + Glucose → Carbon Dioxide + Water + Energy

[Image of Mitochondria diagram]
TEACHER VOICE

"Look at that equation. It is the exact opposite of Photosynthesis. Plants put the energy IN (glucose), we take the energy OUT (ATP)."
