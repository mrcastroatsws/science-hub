MON | CONCEPT 1

Anatomy of a Formula

H2O

Symbol (H): The Element (Hydrogen).

Subscript (2): The Quantity of atoms.

No subscript? It equals 1.

MON | CONCEPT 2

The Big Change

vs

MON | CONCEPT 3

Coefficient vs Subscript

2H2O

Coefficient (2): Multiplies the ENTIRE molecule.

This means 2 separate water molecules.

Total H = 4. Total O = 2.

MON | SCAFFOLD

Polyatomic Ions

Parentheses protect the group.

Ca3(PO4)2

Ca = 3

P = 1 x 2 = 2

O = 4 x 2 = 8

MISCONCEPTION ALERT

Students forget to distribute the outside 2 to the Phosphorus. "It rains on everyone inside the parenthesis."

THU | 3.1.4

Molar Mass

The Weight of the Mole

Concept: Atoms are too small to weigh individually. We weigh them in packs called Moles.

Unit: grams per mole (g/mol)

THU | PROCESS

The Algorithm

1. Find Atomic Mass on Periodic Table.

2. Multiply by the Subscript.

3. Add them all together.
