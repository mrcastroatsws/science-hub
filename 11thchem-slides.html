Core Definitions

Chemistry: The study of composition, structure, and changes of matter.

Chemical: A substance with a definite composition (e.g., Sucrose).

Reactants: Starting materials in a chemical reaction.

CFU: Ask students why sugar is a chemical but "air" is a mixture.

Data Collection

Quantitative Data: Numerical observations (e.g., 25.3 mL).

Inference: A logical interpretation based on prior knowledge and observations.

Activity: "I see bubbles" (Observation) vs "It is producing gas" (Inference).

Organic Chemistry

  • Hydrocarbons: Compounds made of ONLY Carbon and Hydrogen.
  • Alkanes: Saturated hydrocarbons (Single bonds only).
  • Alkyl Group: Formed by removing one Hydrogen from an alkane.
