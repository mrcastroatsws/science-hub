The Science Lab

The Science Lab Status

Southwest School has a Science Lab. However, due to equipment constraints and supply limitations, going to the science lab will be difficult this year.

Therefore, the classroom is the Lab.

Safety rules apply whether we are in the Lab room or at our desks. Respect is non-negotiable.

PPE

Goggles and Aprons and other personal protective equipment must be worn in the lab. There are no exceptions.

Safe Zone

No food. No drinks. No water. No gum. The lab environment must remain uncontaminated at all times.

Protocols

If glass breaks or liquid spills: Do not touch it. Inform the teacher immediately. The teacher handles all cleanup.

Routines & Expectations

MMVP

BEHAVIOR
  • Movement: Seated or standing?
  • Materials: Hands empty or writing?
  • Voice: Level 0 (Silent) to 3 (Presenting)
  • Participation: Active engagement only.

At-Bats

PRACTICE

An "At-Bat" is independent and silent practice. You are testing your own brain, not your classmates'.

Turn & Talk

DISCOURSE

Eye contact is key. Turn your body, Partner A speaks, Partner B listens, then switch.

Cold Call

ACCOUNTABILITY

Everyone gets a chance. You must always be ready to contribute, even if your hand isn't up.
